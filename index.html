About Bakare

Bakare Oladapo Emmanuel is a mathematics educator, EdTech developer, and web developer working at the intersection of education and technology. Through his D-Maths Tuition Centre brand and his role at Whyte Pyramid Academy (WPA), he has built an extensive library of interactive tools — CBT assessment portals, animated classroom lessons, and full-stack web platforms — serving students across both the UK and Nigerian curricula.

His development stack spans JavaScript, PHP, Google Apps Script, and Next.js, always in service of one goal: making quality education accessible through technology that actually works in the classroom. He has designed student management systems, online tutoring platforms, and gamified learning experiences that thousands of learners interact with weekly.

A committed Christian, Bakare brings the same intentionality into every space — including this one. At Nea Poreia, he speaks not just to career, but to calling: what it looks like to build something purposeful with the skills God has given you, one step at a time.
